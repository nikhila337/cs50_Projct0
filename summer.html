10 Best Summer Anime 2020 to Watch

  1. Haikyuu!!To the Top:Part II

    2. After securing the Miyagi Prefecture qualification for the National spring volleyball tournament, Hinata Shoyo’s journey with the Karasuno volleyball team continues onto the national stage of the spring volleyball tournament. Haikyuu season 4 or Haikyuu!! To the Top begins with Kageyama Tobio receiving an invitation for a training camp for the future Japan national athletes. Meanwhile, Tsukishima received an invitation for a training camp for talented players in Miyagi Prefecture, in other words, an imitation of the national training camp. However, it’s sole purpose is to train the talented players from Miyagi Prefecture. Well, that’s the list of the best summer anime 2020 coming up this summer. Wait for another anime discussion on Dunia Games.

  2. Re:Zero kara Hajimeru Isekai Seikatsu Season 2

    3. Subaru Natsuki got himself suddenly moved to another world. Without no idea of who moved him, Subaru befriended a silver-haired half-elf girl named Emilia. When he and the girl mysteriously got killed, Subaru wakes up and realizes that he’s been granted the ability "Return by Death", allowing him to return to the time before his demise. Season 2 of Re:Zero is a sequel to Subaru’s adventure in his effort to save Emilia.

  3. Mahouka Koukou no Rettousei: Raihousha-hen

    4. Miyuki’s classmate, Shizuku Kitayama is on her way to study abroad. For magicians, it’s normally impossible since allowing the genes of someone who can use magic outside their home country’s borders is the same as giving up national secrets.

  4. Higurashi no Naku Koro ni

    5. In June 1983, a tragic event took place. In a dark room of the night, a man with a baseball bat beat two girls in their school uniform to death. He struck the bat with all his might to the victim’s head, arms, and legs until they’re covered in blood and their bones crushed. Finished with his victims, with a heavy breath, he stood still with a devilish look on his face.

  5. Dungeon ni Deai wo Motomeru no wa Machigatteiru Darou ka III

    6. Dungeon ni Deai wo Motomeru no wa Machigatteiru Darou ka, better known as DanMachi has entered its third season, and the season 3 of this anime series will be released this summer. Follow Cranel Bell and Hestia’s adventure in DanMachi III!

  6. Kanojo, Okarishimasu

    7. Kinoshita Kazuya is a college student who just got dumped by his girlfriend who left him for another guy. Feeling sad and desperate, he decides to use an application called Diamond to rent Mizuhara Chizuru—a rental girlfriend, to make himself feel better. From their first encounter, she seemed to be the perfect girl for him.

  7. Assault Lily Bouquet

    8. In the near future earth, humanity is crumbling and facing a mysterious creature called “Huge”. The world unites against the Huge and develops the anti-weapon “CHARM” (Counter Huge Arms) by combining both science and magic. CHARM works best when used by teenage girls, and those who use the weapons are revered as “Lilies”. The Lilies are trained at military academies called “Gardens”, which also serves as bases to protect and advise people. This is a story about girls who aspire to become Lilies in order to protect the earth.

  8. Monster Musume no Oishasan

    9. In the town of Lindworm, where monsters and humans coexist, Dr. Glenn runs a clinic for monster girls with a Lamia assistant, Sapphee. From receiving a marriage proposal from a battle-wounded centaur, touching a mermaid’s wound, or sewing a Golem’s wound, Dr. Glenn does his job well and full of confidence.

  9. Maou Gakuin no Futekigousha

    10. Despite having a strong urge for destruction, the demon lord is getting tired of all the bloodshed and chaos. When Anoth reincarnates for a peaceful life, he goes to school with his descendants in an old palace 2,000 years later. In a totally different time, no one can measure Anoth’s true power.

  10. Gibiate

    11. The story sets in Japan, in the year 2030, where a virus outbreak has hit the whole world. After being infected by the virus, the afflicted become monsters and they transform, based on their ages, genders, and nationalities. The virus is called "Gibier". A Samurai and a Shinobi from the Edo period have emerged from the post-apocalyptic ruins, helping a doctor who tries to find a cure for "Gibier".
